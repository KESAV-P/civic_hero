# Civic Hero - Demo Video Script

**Tone**: Professional, Energetic, and Solution-Oriented.
**Total Duration**: Approx. 2-3 Minutes.

---

### **Scene 1: Introduction (Landing Page)**
**(Action: Start on the Home Page. Scroll slowly through the hero section and features.)**

**Voiceover**:
"Hello and welcome. Today, we are proud to present **Civic Hero**, a next-generation civic engagement platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local administration.

In our rapidly growing cities, reporting civic issues like potholes, garbage, or faulty streetlights has always been a challenge. Civic Hero changes that."

---

### **Scene 2: Citizen Reporting (User Flow)**
**(Action: Click 'Start Reporting Now'. Open the Login Modal. Select 'Citizen', enter a dummy number, OTP, and login.)**

**Voiceover**:
"Let's see how easy it is for a citizen using the app. With a simple, secure login via mobile number, any resident can access the platform instantly."

**(Action: On the Dashboard, click 'Report Issue'. Show the new detailed list of categories.)**

**Voiceover**:
"Reporting an issue takes seconds. Whether it’s a **Pothole**, **Garbage Overflow**, or a **Broken Streetlight**, we cover every major civic problem.

Watch this—when I click 'Capture Location'..."

**(Action: Click 'Capture Location'. Allow GPS permission if asked. Show the address appearing.)**

**Voiceover**:
"...the system uses real-time GPS to pinpoint exactly where the problem is. No more vague addresses. The user can simply snap a photo, add a note, and hit Submit."

**(Action: Click 'Submit Complaint'. Show the success alert with the Auto-Assigned Department.)**

**Voiceover**:
"And here is the magic. The moment I submit, Civic Hero's intelligent backend **automatically routes** the complaint to the correct department—Roads, Sanitation, or Electrical—without any manual intervention."

---

### **Scene 3: Admin Command Center (Admin Flow)**
**(Action: Log out. Open Login Modal again. Select 'Admin' tab. Log in as Admin.)**

**Voiceover**:
"Now, let's switch to the Administration view. This is the **Admin Command Center**."

**(Action: Show the Dashboard stats, the AI Insights box, and the complaint list.)**

**Voiceover**:
"Admins get a bird’s-eye view of the city. At the top, our **Gemini-powered AI Intelligence Layer** analyzes incoming data to provide real-time summaries—telling officials exactly what needs attention today."

**(Action: Switch to 'Map Visualization' mode. Check 'Show Heatmap'.)**

**Voiceover**:
"With our **interactive Map Visualization**, officials can see complaints geographically. We can even toggle a **Heatmap** to identify high-density 'Red Zones' where infrastructure is failing repeatedly."

---

### **Scene 4: Resolving an Issue**
**(Action: Switch back to List View. Filter by 'Submitted'. Click 'Manage' on a complaint.)**

**Voiceover**:
"When an admin clicks 'Manage', they see the full picture—the photo, the exact location on the map, and the user's details."

**(Action: Show the details. Change Status to 'In Progress' or 'Resolved'. Click Update.)**

**Voiceover**:
"They can update the status in real-time. As soon as this is marked 'Resolved', the citizen gets notified instantly. This closes the loop of trust."

---

### **Scene 5: Conclusion**
**(Action: meaningful pause on the Dashboard or Home Page.)**

**Voiceover**:
"Civic Hero isn't just an app; it's a transparency engine. By combining easy reporting, automated routing, and AI-driven insights, we are building smarter, safer, and cleaner cities for everyone.

Thank you."
